Our mission at The Roof Guys is simple. We want to make the experience of improving your home as smooth as possible. We achieve this by being unmatched in responsiveness to our customers, the quality of our work, and the support we offer before, during, and after the job.

We have plenty of options for your home. Whether you have asphalt shingles, metal, wood, or membrane on your roof, we have you covered. Not only that, we can also repair things that have gone awry such as swamp coolers or vents. Check out our services below and see what we can do for you.

Our Services

Bids and Estimates

We offer bids, estimates, and second opinions on all roofs of all varieties absolutely free of charge. We want to help you get the information you need to make the best choice possible for your roof and would be happy to come take a look!

Roof Replacement

We replace roofs of all varieties. Shingles, metal standing seam, metal shake, rolled flat roofing, membrane roofing of all types, and all types of wooden and plastic shakes. If you have a roof, we can repair or replace it!

Roof Repairs

We can repair any damage that may come to your roof. Shingles, fans, vents, swamp coolers, plywood, or anything else that may show cause for concern.

Roof Maintenance

We offer yearly and monthly roof upkeep and maintenance plans. For those people who have debris-producing trees near their roof that clog their gutters, this is an ideal plan to keep water going down the drain and off the roof. Payment can be either on a service by service basis, or once for the year and we will stop by consistently to ensure your house is still looking great.
